## 🎭 Animations

### Entrance Animations
- **Card**: Slides up from bottom (0.6s)
- **Icon**: Scales in with bounce (0.6s)
- **Checkmark**: Rotates and scales (0.8s)
- **Title**: Fades in from below (0.6s)
- **Content**: Staggered fade-in (0.6-0.8s)

### Interactive Animations
- **Pulse Ring**: Continuous pulse around icon
- **Button Hover**: Lift effect with shadow
- **Copy Button**: Color change on success
- **All Transitions**: Smooth cubic-bezier easing
